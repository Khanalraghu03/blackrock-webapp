Penny Stocks

Penny stocks are very risky. Most of the people fear investing their money on stocks because of the stories they have heard from people who invest in penny stocks. Penny stocks are like playing a lottery, you could lose all you money in few hours or make lot of money within in a few hours. Those who invest in penny stocks are usually are under assumptions instead of being lead by "facts" about companies' product, services, reputation and so on. A lot of people get into penny stocks and end of losing a lot of money.

What is considered a penny stocks?

Any stock that is $5 or less of worth is considered a penny stock. They can sometimes go way up or come way back down. Most of the time, these stocks are manipulated and not really speculated.

Investing in big company

Investing in companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Google etc are way less risky. By evaluating the company Microsoft, it has at least 10% return on investment per year. If you compare that to your regular saving account, which is usually 0.03%. It is way wiser to invest than to save your money on savings account.

Compound Interest

Compound interest is the addition of interest to the principal sum of a loan or deposit, or in other words, interest on interest. It is the result of reinvesting interest, rather than paying it out, so that interest in the next period is then earned on the principal sum plus previously accumulated interest.

Do not invest any of your money until you fully understand how investing works. The purpose of this app is to provide education on investing than rushing into to making an investments.
